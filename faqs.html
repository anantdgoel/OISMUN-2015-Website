  • What is Model United Nations?

    A Model United Nations is an authentic simulation of the U.N. General Assembly and other multilateral bodies widely practised all over the world.

  • What is Oberoi International School Model United Nations (OISMUN)?

    It is an event organized by Oberoi International School. It is a replication of the MUN. It strives to foster a constructive forum for open debate on complex global issues amongst the delegates.

  • Who is a MUN delegate?

    A student who assumes the role of an ambassador. He/She represents the country that has been assigned to him/her for all matters at the MUN. He/She gets involved in debates, discussions for his/her country as in the actual United Nations.

  • Who can participate in this MUN?

    Students belonging to Government recognised schools and colleges, studying in Grade 9 in August 2015 and above can participate in the conference. Recommended age: 14 to 18 years.

  • Do I get to choose the committee I would like to be in?

    The delegates would mention their committee preference in their form but their final decision on the committee allocations lies with the Executive Board of the MUN.

  • Is attendance taken in Committee sessions?

    Yes- for every Committee session.

  • What is the dress code for delegates?

    The delegates need to wear formal western business wear at all times of the conference.

  • What will be the gadgets allowed during the conference?

    The delegates will be allowed to use their laptops/ iPads /tablets during the conference and the committee sessions. The internet facility will be provided by the school. Usage of mobile phones during the conference is prohibited. Mobiles will be confiscated if the delegate is caught using their phones.

  • What will be included in the delegate kit?

    The delegate kit would include an ID card, the pre-conference press release, a placard for each delegate for her/his country, a notepad, a pen, a conference schedule, a conference map, conference information and the delegate code of conduct.

  • Is there a code of conduct needs to be followed?

    Yes, there is a code of conduct which needs to be followed. This will be given to the delegates along with their delegate kit.

  • When is the MUN?

    The MUN will be held on 9th and 10th October, 2015 at Oberoi International School, Goregaon, Mumbai.

  • Do I get a certificate for attending the conference?

    Yes. The delegates would get a certificate for attending all the sessions.
