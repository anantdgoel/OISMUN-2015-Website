Letter from Secretary-General


Dear all,
We, the members of the Executive Board at Oberoi International School’s Model United Nations conference, welcome you to another much-awaited year of animated debate on the 9th and 10th of October at our campus.
OISMUN is a journey of self-discovery, a wormhole to the insightful perceptions of the youth - their hopes, beliefs and ideologies. It is a platform where we engage and encourage you to go beyond mere discussion, to undertake your responsibility in shaping tomorrow’s world. We wish to guide you in your endeavors to bring peace and harmony to the world that is plagued by ubiquitous problems, each with their own convoluted complications. In order to achieve this, we will facilitate a conducive forum to foster spirited dialogue to come to harmonious solutions of the highest standards.
Our five committees will be addressing critical issues of today, that yearn for the outlooks of the youngest and the brightest to warrant a better future. Thought-provoking agendas range from Border Disputes in the Western Sahara to the Universal Legalization of Homosexuality - issues that are being discussed in the actual UN.
Our goal is to empower you to lead and build bridges, to learn and connect with unique individuals, and to network into a wider spectrum - all while having fun.
This could be your chance to shine. We leave the fate of the world in your hands.
OISMUN 2015.
It’s time to begin.

Ayan Saraf,
Secretary-General
